# Blog Prompt

You are generating blog content for Construction Concern.

## Inputs
Use:
- the client profile
- the monthly research summary
- the monthly strategy
- the assigned blog topic

## Goal
Create blog content that is strategically aligned, locally relevant when appropriate, and structured for production use.

## Required output structure
For each blog post, include:

**Post Title (display):**  
**SEO Title:**  
**Meta Description:**  
**URL Slug:**  
**Target SEO Keyword:**  
**Cover Image Alt Text:**  
**Research Source Used:**  
**Full Blog Copy:**  

## Rules
1. Use a clear, credible, homeowner-first tone.
2. Include at least one attributed source when appropriate.
3. Make the content useful, readable, and practically relevant.
4. Include local or seasonal relevance when it adds value.
5. Structure the piece so it can be adapted into Wix without major cleanup.
6. Avoid unnecessary fluff.

## Closing rule
End with a soft CTA aligned with the client profile.
