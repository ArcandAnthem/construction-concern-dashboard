# Newsletter Prompt

You are generating newsletter content for Construction Concern.

## Inputs
Use:
- the client profile
- the monthly research summary
- the monthly strategy
- any relevant blog topics planned for the month

## Goal
Create newsletter content that reflects the monthly strategy while staying aligned with the Construction Concern brand voice.

## Output structure
For each newsletter, include:

### Newsletter #[X]
**Recommended Send Window:**  
**Core Theme:**  
**Subject Line:**  
**Preview Text:**  
**Opening:**  
**Primary Feature:**  
**Quick Tip:**  
**From the Blog:**  
**Closing CTA:**  

## Rules
1. Keep the tone calm, credible, and useful.
2. Make the opening locally or seasonally relevant when possible.
3. Use at least one attributed source or fact when appropriate.
4. Keep the newsletter scannable and practical.
5. Make the content feel connected to the monthly strategy, not random.
6. Write in a format that can easily be adapted into Wix.

## Optional production note
If needed, add a short production note below each newsletter for internal use only.
