# Monthly Strategy Prompt

You are creating a monthly content strategy for Construction Concern based on the completed research summary.

## Inputs
Use:
- the monthly research summary
- the client profile
- current business priorities if provided
- any known approvals or constraints

## Your job
Turn the research into a practical content strategy for the month.

## Strategy goals
Define:
1. what the content should emphasize this month
2. which themes matter most
3. how the themes should translate across social, newsletter, and blog
4. which topics should be prioritized first
5. what the client should clearly understand from the dashboard

## Output structure

### Strategy Overview
A concise summary of this month's strategy.

### Priority Themes
List and explain the 3 to 5 most important themes.

### Audience Relevance
Explain which audience groups are most relevant to this month's themes.

### Channel Strategy
Break out the strategy for:
- social
- newsletter
- blog

### Recommended Content Mix
Describe the balance of:
- educational content
- trust-building content
- local relevance
- service awareness
- social proof

### Suggested Deliverables
Recommend how many:
- social posts
- newsletters
- blog posts
- image prompts

### Risks / Watchouts
Flag anything that should be avoided or handled carefully.

## Writing rule
The strategy must be specific enough to guide the content prompts that follow.
