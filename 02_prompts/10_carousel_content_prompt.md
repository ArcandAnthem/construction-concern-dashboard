# Carousel Content Prompt

You are generating carousel (multi-slide) social posts for Construction Concern.

## Purpose

Carousel posts are Instagram's second-highest-performing format after Reels. Each swipe counts as an engagement signal, which increases algorithmic distribution. On Facebook, multi-image posts perform similarly. Carousels are the primary strategy for growing reach and engagement without video content.

This prompt generates slide-by-slide content ready to be handed to a designer or used with Canva templates.

## Inputs

Add to context before running this prompt:
- `01_config/client_profile.md`
- the monthly research summary
- the monthly social strategy
- any specific topics or post ideas to develop as carousels

## When to use carousels

Use carousel format for:
- List posts ("5 signs your roof needs attention")
- Process posts ("What to expect during a window replacement")
- Before/after reveals (if client photos are available)
- Myth vs. fact comparisons
- Seasonal checklists ("Your fall home checklist for SoCal homeowners")
- Cost transparency breakdowns ("Here's what affects the price of new siding")
- FAQ formats ("Your top questions about [service] — answered")

## Slide structure

Every carousel must follow this structure:

**Slide 1 — Hook slide**
- Big, bold hook text (use the Hook Writing Prompt format)
- Minimal design — one strong headline, no clutter
- Should stop the scroll on its own

**Slides 2–5 (or 2–7 for longer carousels)**
- One point per slide
- Keep text to 1–2 short lines per slide
- Each slide should feel like a new reveal — not a continuation of a paragraph
- Add a brief supporting sentence if needed (not required on every slide)

**Final slide — CTA slide**
- A clear, warm call to action
- Use one of the approved CTAs from the client profile:
  - "Start with a conversation."
  - "Request your free estimate."
  - "Visit constructionconcern.com/free-estimate"
- Include the business name and/or handle

## Output format

For each carousel, deliver:

### Carousel: [Topic Title]
**Platform:** [Instagram / Facebook / LinkedIn]
**Hook (Slide 1 text):** [Hook headline]
**Caption (for the post itself):** [Full caption with hashtags — written as if the person doesn't swipe]

**Slides:**
- Slide 1: [Headline text]
- Slide 2: [Point + supporting line]
- Slide 3: [Point + supporting line]
- Slide 4: [Point + supporting line]
- Slide 5: [Point + supporting line]
- Slide 6 (if applicable): [Point + supporting line]
- Final slide: [CTA text]

**Design note:** [One sentence on visual direction — warm tones, clean layout, etc.]

## Rules

1. Each slide must work as a standalone visual — not dependent on reading the previous slide.
2. Avoid walls of text — this is not a blog post in slides.
3. The hook slide is the most important — spend the most time on it.
4. The final CTA slide must always include a clear next step.
5. Adapt slide count to the topic — not every carousel needs 7 slides. 4–5 focused slides beats 8 padded ones.
6. Do not include image descriptions inside the caption text.
7. Write the caption assuming someone sees only Slide 1 before deciding to swipe — the caption must work independently of the slides.
