# Social Strategy — Construction Concern — June 2026

## Posting Strategy for the Month

June strategy has one primary objective: convert existing reach into engagement. May analytics confirmed that posts are being seen (190–345 people per post on Facebook organically) but generating near-zero engagement. The root cause is hook failure — posts opened with lifestyle descriptions or brand statements that do not stop a scroll. Every June post opens with a list, problem, question, or local hook. No exceptions.

Secondary objective: launch carousels on Instagram. Zero carousels have been published in any prior month. June deploys the first 3. This is the primary Reels alternative available without video content.

All posts: 3–4 PM Pacific. Best days: Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday (mix in 1–2 weekend posts).

**Target audience rotation:**
- Budget Optimizers: cost savings, ROI, LADWP energy bills, bundled upgrades
- Style Nesters: curb appeal, before/after, seasonal fresh look
- Property Managers: multi-unit considerations, exterior durability, tenant comfort

---

## Research Guiding the Posting Strategy

- **Hook format is the #1 lever.** List-format and problem-hook posts outperformed lifestyle openers 3:1 in reach in May. Applying to 100% of June posts.
- **LADWP energy angle has proven reach.** The post connecting windows to LADWP cooling costs achieved 302 views — third highest of May. Summer billing season peaks in June. Recurs throughout summer.
- **Siding/exterior was the top-reach post.** "Siding affects more than curb appeal" = 345 views. Fire season in June adds a second strong reason to cover exterior materials.
- **Carousels = untapped.** Instagram carousels outperform single images for saves and shares. First deployment in June.
- **Email list subject lines: list format wins.** 42% open rate vs 30% — 12-point lift. Same rule applied to social hooks.
- **No messages received on any platform.** Action items shift to outbound engagement (commenting on local accounts, not replying to inbound).
