What the Monthly Research Shows
Proven Topic Lane

The clearest non-brand content winner is still fire safety and boxed-eaves education, led by the boxed-eaves blog post.

Discovery Pattern

Direct traffic and Google search are driving discovery more than social referral, so July strategy follows search demand first.

Trust-Page Behavior

Contact and About pages are attracting stronger traffic than many individual service pages, which means reassurance is a key step before estimate intent.
