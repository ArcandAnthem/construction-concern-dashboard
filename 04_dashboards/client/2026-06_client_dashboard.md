## What the Research Tells Us About June

June is a high-relevance month for your audience. Here's what we found:

**Summer energy bills are top of mind.** LADWP rates go up in summer, and homeowners in the Santa Clarita area notice. Our posts connecting windows to lower cooling costs consistently outperformed others in May — we're doubling down on this angle.

**Fire season is starting.** LA County and Ventura County are entering fire season, and homeowners in Santa Clarita and Newhall are aware of it. We're publishing content about protecting a home's exterior for fire safety — this is a topic we know drives traffic from search (our fire safety blog from January has been the top-performing post on your site all year).

**Carousels are the format we haven't tried yet.** Instagram carousels — posts with multiple slides — consistently outperform single images. We haven't used them yet. June is the month we launch them, and it should make a noticeable difference in how far posts travel.

## Our Strategy for June

The two main moves this month:

1. **Fix the first line.** May showed that posts were reaching people — the problem was that they weren't stopping anyone. We're rewriting how posts open: starting with a problem, a list, or a local question instead of a description or brand statement. This is the highest-leverage change we can make.

2. **Launch carousels on Instagram.** Three posts this month will use the multi-slide carousel format. These tend to get saved and shared — both of which help grow the account.

## Your Action Items This Month

1. **Share recent project photos.** Before/after photos from real jobs are the most powerful content we can publish. Even phone photos from a completed project make a big difference. Can you send us 3–5 photos from a recent job?

2. **Ask your customers to share.** After each completed job, if the conversation feels right, ask the customer to post about it or tag Construction Concern. Even one organic customer post can reach more people than a week of brand posts.

3. **Forward the newsletter to one person.** For each newsletter you send to your own contacts, forward it to one person who owns a home in your service area. This is the fastest way to grow the list outside of social media.

4. **Let us know about any June promotions or specials.** If you're running any summer offers, we'll weave them into the newsletter and social content.
